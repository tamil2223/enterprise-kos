Reranking (cross-encoder) (golden corpus)

Hybrid retrieval often returns “pretty good” candidates but still mixes in weak matches.

A cross-encoder reranker improves precision by scoring (query, passage) pairs more accurately than embedding similarity or BM25 alone.

In KOS, reranking is a Phase v2 upgrade: it sits after candidate retrieval and before the MCP gateway finalizes the context bundle.
