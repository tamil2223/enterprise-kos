Observability for agent systems (golden corpus)

Agent systems fail in subtle ways: retrieval drift, truncation, tool errors, and latency spikes.

KOS observability should capture:
- Step traces (router/researcher/synthesizer inputs + outputs)
- Retrieval metrics (hit rate, top-k distribution, context budget usage)
- Token/latency metrics per step and per endpoint

In Phase v2, this can be wired to LangSmith and/or Prometheus.
